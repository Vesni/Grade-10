Server Rules

  1. No griefing or stealing from other players under any circumstances.
  2. Respect all players, moderators, and admins — harassment or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.
  3. No cheating, X-ray resource packs, or exploit abuse — play fair.
  4. Keep chat clean: no spam, no offensive language, no hate speech.
  5. Don’t advertise other servers, Discords, or communities here without permission.
  6. Report any bugs or issues responsibly — abuse will lead to a ban.
  7. Do not build near other players’ bases without permission.
  8. PvP is only allowed if both players agree — no random killing.
  9. Do not use alternate accounts to bypass bans or mutes.
  10. Use common sense — if you think it might be against the rules, ask staff first.
  11. Listen to staff instructions at all times — repeated rule breaking will lead to permanent bans.
